# InterviewLens — Product Overview

## Product Identity

**Name:** InterviewLens  
**Tagline:** See every candidate clearly. Hire with confidence.  
**Type:** Internal SaaS — AI-powered interview video analysis platform  
**Positioning:** The most reliable way for hiring teams to surface objective insights from interview recordings — faster, fairer, and at any scale.

---

## What It Is

InterviewLens is a web application that lets companies upload recorded interview videos and receive automated, AI-generated analysis for each candidate. The platform transcribes interviews, scores candidates on standardised criteria, extracts key signals (keywords, sentiment, engagement), and produces shareable summaries — eliminating hours of manual replay and note-taking.

It is designed for internal use by a company's hiring team: HR managers, hiring managers, and recruiters collaborate within a shared workspace.

---

## The Problem It Solves

- **Time drain:** Reviewing interview recordings manually consumes 4–6 hours per hiring round, per recruiter.
- **Inconsistency:** Different interviewers evaluate the same candidate against different mental models, making comparisons unreliable.
- **Missed signals:** Rushed note-taking causes evaluators to miss critical moments — hesitations, enthusiasm, depth of answer.
- **Hard to scale:** As candidate volume grows, manual review creates a bottleneck that prevents rapid, high-quality hiring.

---

## Core Solution

InterviewLens processes every uploaded interview video automatically:

1. Transcribes audio with speaker separation and timestamps.
2. Scores candidates on clarity, confidence, communication quality, and role-specific competency signals.
3. Detects keywords and topic coverage aligned to the job requirements.
4. Analyses sentiment and engagement patterns across the interview.
5. Generates a structured candidate summary with top insights and a ranked score.
6. Enables side-by-side comparison of multiple candidates on the same criteria.
7. Produces shareable reports for team discussion and hiring decisions.

---

## Target Users & Personas

| Persona | Role | Primary Need |
|---|---|---|
| **HR Manager** | Owns the hiring process end-to-end | Standardise evaluation, reduce time-to-hire, reduce bias |
| **Hiring Manager** | Makes final candidate decisions | Fast, credible summary of each candidate without re-watching videos |
| **Recruiter / Talent Acquisition** | Manages pipeline volume | Quickly triage large candidate pools and surface the top tier |

**Target organisation:** Mid-market to enterprise companies with active, recurring hiring programmes.

---

## Key Features

1. **Video Upload & Processing** — Bulk upload, drag-and-drop, and cloud storage integration. Supports all major video formats.
2. **AI Transcription** — Accurate, speaker-separated transcripts with timestamped highlights for rapid review.
3. **Candidate Scoring** — Automated scores for clarity, confidence, communication, and role-specific competencies on a consistent rubric.
4. **Keyword & Topic Detection** — Surface skill signals and topic coverage relevant to each job description.
5. **Sentiment & Engagement Analysis** — Track emotional tone, enthusiasm, and response quality throughout the interview.
6. **Side-by-Side Candidate Comparison** — Compare multiple candidates on the same criteria for confident shortlisting.
7. **Automated Summaries** — Structured, AI-written candidate summaries with strengths, flags, and a ranked recommendation.
8. **Reports & Team Collaboration** — Shareable reports, team comments, and collaborative review in a shared workspace.

---

## Measurable Outcomes

- **70%** reduction in manual interview review time per hiring cycle
- **3×** faster candidate shortlisting with AI-ranked summaries
- **100%** consistent scoring criteria across all interviewers and roles
- Fewer mis-hires and lower cost-per-hire through structured, objective analysis
